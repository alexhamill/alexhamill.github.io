

# ideal gas equation

## $PV=nRT$

$n = moles$

$P = Presure$

$V = Volume$

$R = idealgasconstent = 8.314 joules * mol^-1 * K^-1 = 0.08206 atm * K^-1 * mol^-1 =
62.36 L * torr * mol^-1 * K^-1$

$n=\frac{PV}{RT}$
