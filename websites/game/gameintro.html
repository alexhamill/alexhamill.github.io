Alex's Game

Welcome to the game!

The goal is to gain score by shooting the red boxes but you have to watch out because your arrows might come back to hit you

you can charge a shot by holding left click, q or space and releasing to fire

You can pause the game by pressing 'p' or the pausing button in the top right

you can open the upgrade menu by pressing 'u' or the arrow at the top of the screen

you can use your coins to buy a shield or a turret in the store
