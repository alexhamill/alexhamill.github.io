stuffing is a staple of the thanksgiving time and as long been my favrotie dish. Sometimes I strugle to find a good vegitarian stuffing so here if the recipy you should you this thanksgiving. This recipie feeds 20-30 so it for a bigger group of people.

ingredient: Amount:
Peppridge farm stuffing Two 14 oz bags
Celery Hearts 16 oz
yellow onions 2 big ones
un-chopped fresh peeled garlic 32 oz
butter 3/4 lb
leeks one big package
vegetable broth 32 oz box
  1. Empty stuffing into a big kettle.
  2. Dice onions.
  3. in 1/4 lb butter, saute the onions until clear.
  4. add the ontions to the stuffing.
  5. Dice celery.
  6. in 1/4 lb butter, saute the celery until tender.
  7. add the celery to the stuffing.
  8. mince the garlic in a food processor.
  9. cut leeks into 1/4" rings.
  10. in 1/4 lb butter, saute the leeks and garlic lightly
  11. add leeks and garlic to stuffing
  12. boil the broth then add it to the stuffing and stir
  13. Transfer to casserole dishes and bake at 350 for about a hour
