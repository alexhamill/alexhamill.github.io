Best Yams with Cranberries and Apples Recipe

This recipe is a staple of my thanksgiving dinner at my grandmother's house. I never liked it very much but it is still a part of every thanksgiving for me. My mom said that it was tangy and sweet in just the right way. A tip is to not mix the fruits too much because you don't want to turn them too much. This recipe serves 6-8 people and takes about 1 hour and 30 mins to make.

Ingrediends:

Ingredient

Amount
Large peeled and diced apples 1
cups of raw crainberries 1
Large peeled yams 4
cups of rasins 1/2
Tbps sugar 2
Tbps margarine 1-2
cups of orange juice 1/2

Steps:

  1. cut peeled yams into one inch chunks.
  2. put yam cubes into a large baking dish.
  3. put diced apples crainberrys and raisins into the dish.
  4. spinkle with sugar or other sweetene.
  5. pour orange juice all over.
  6. cover and bake at 350 for one hour and 15 minutes.
