Best Yams with Cranberries and Apples Recipe

This recipie is a staple of your thanksgiving dinner and goes amaizing with all other iconic thanksgiving dishes

recipe

Ingredient

Amount
Large peeled and diced apples 1
cups of raw crainberries 1
Large peeled yams 4
cups of rasins 1/2
Tbps sugar 2
Tbps margarine 1-2
cups of orange juice 1/2
  1. cut peeled yams into one inch chunks
  2. put yam cubes into a large baking dish
  3. put diced apples crainberrys and raisins into the dish
  4. spinkle with sugar or other sweetener
  5. pour orange juice all over
  6. cover and bake at 350 for one hour and 15 minutes
